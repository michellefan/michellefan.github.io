Courses: Structure and Interpretation of Computer Programs (CS 61A), Data Structures (CS 61B), Machine Structures (CS 61C), Designing Information Devices and Systems(EE 16A), Web Design DeCal, Discrete Mathematics and Probability (CS 70), Linear Algebra and Differential Equations(MATH 54)
